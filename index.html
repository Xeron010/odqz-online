[ 04 ] — Approach

How we work.

01

Listening,
before drawing.

Every engagement opens with a structured discovery phase — usually two to three weeks of stakeholder conversations, audit work, and reading. We've never solved a brand problem we didn't first take the time to understand.

02

Strategy
as a written thing.

We document. Positioning, audience, voice, visual direction — all of it written down before a single pixel moves. Strategy that lives only in someone's head doesn't survive the project.

03

Craft, in
a slow hand.

We work in small teams of two to three on each engagement. No account managers, no telephone. The people designing the work are the people you talk to about it. That's not a feature — it's the only way we know how to be good.

04

Care
that continues.

Most projects roll into a retainer. New brand systems need maintenance, websites need iteration, growth needs attention. We stay close to the brands we launch — that's where most of our time actually goes.

[ 05 ] — Studio

A small studio, by design.

We started ODQZ in 2024 because the work we wanted to do — slow, considered, in-house from strategy through code — wasn't happening at the agencies we'd worked at before.

Today we're a team of six designers, developers, and writers based out of a quiet studio in Taunsa Sharif, working with clients across Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, and abroad. We work with a deliberately small client list — typically four to six concurrent engagements — split between project work and ongoing retainers.

Most of our clients come through referral. We turn down more work than we take. That isn't false modesty; it's how we keep the quality where we want it.

If you're a founder, an in-house brand lead, or an operator at a company that takes its work seriously, we'd like to hear from you.

[ 10 ] — Refund & Cancellation

Refunds, cancellations & what to expect.

Last updated: 1 May 2026 — applies to all paid engagements with ODQZ Studio

Because we provide custom professional services rather than physical products, our refund policy reflects how design and development work is typically billed and delivered.

1. Project deposits

The 50% deposit required to begin a project is partially refundable if the client cancels before any creative or development work has commenced. In that case, an administrative charge of PKR 15,000 will be retained to cover the discovery call, scoping, and account setup, and the remainder will be returned within 7 working days.

2. After work has commenced

Once design or development work has begun on the engagement, the deposit becomes non-refundable. If the project is cancelled mid-way, the client is invoiced for any work completed beyond the deposit value, calculated at our standard hourly rate of PKR 8,000 per hour. All deliverables produced up to that point are handed over upon settlement.

3. Retainer cancellation

Monthly retainers require a six-month minimum commitment. After that period, retainers may be cancelled with 30 days written notice. No refunds are issued for the current month if cancellation is requested mid-month; the retainer continues for the remainder of that billing cycle, after which it ends.

4. Dissatisfaction with deliverables

Each engagement includes a defined number of revision rounds. If after using the included revisions the client is still dissatisfied, we will work with them in good faith to either revise the deliverable further at our expense, agree on a partial refund, or terminate the engagement amicably. We have always preferred a frank conversation to a contractual dispute.

5. Online payments & chargebacks

Refunds for payments made via PayFast (cards, RAAST, JazzCash, Easypaisa) are processed back to the original payment method within 7 to 14 working days, subject to PayFast's processing timelines. The client agrees not to initiate a chargeback for any work that has been delivered and accepted in line with the SOW.

6. How to request a refund or cancellation

Any refund or cancellation request should be raised in writing to billing@odqz.online with the engagement reference number and the reason for the request. We commit to acknowledging within 48 working hours and resolving within 10 working days where possible.

[ 11 ] — Service Delivery

How services are delivered.

Last updated: 1 May 2026 — service delivery and shipping policy

ODQZ delivers digital design and development services. We do not ship physical goods, so the standard concept of postal "shipping" does not apply. The following describes how our service deliverables are made available to clients.

1. Engagement timelines

Every engagement begins with a written Statement of Work that includes a project timeline with key milestones. Typical timelines are:

  • Brand identity engagement — 6 to 8 weeks
  • Marketing website — 8 to 12 weeks
  • E-commerce or product UI — 12 to 16 weeks
  • Retainer engagements — ongoing, monthly cycles
  • One-off services (audits, refreshes) — 2 to 4 weeks

2. Delivery channels

All work products are delivered digitally. Specifically:

  • Design files via Figma share links or direct download from a private cloud folder
  • Brand guidelines as a downloadable PDF document
  • Source files and assets via a zipped archive on Google Drive or Dropbox
  • Websites deployed directly to the client's hosting environment with admin handoff
  • Retainer outputs delivered through a shared monthly folder and weekly status emails

3. Communication during the engagement

Throughout the engagement, the assigned project lead is available via email and scheduled video calls. We do not communicate via WhatsApp during working hours unless specifically requested by the client. Standard response time is one working day.

4. Working hours & holidays

Our working hours are Monday to Friday, 09:30 to 18:30 Pakistan Standard Time (PKT). We observe Pakistani public holidays, which may extend timelines by one or two days during major events such as Eid-ul-Fitr, Eid-ul-Adha, and Independence Day. Clients are notified in writing in advance of any holiday-related delays.

5. Hosting & ongoing access

For website projects, the client owns and pays for their own hosting and domain. We assist with setup but do not host client websites on our infrastructure unless a hosting retainer is in place.

6. Acceptance & handoff

Each milestone deliverable is reviewed by the client. The client has 7 working days to provide feedback or accept the deliverable. If no response is received within 7 working days, the deliverable is considered accepted by default and the engagement moves to the next milestone.

7. Post-delivery support

All projects include 30 days of post-delivery support for bug fixes and minor tweaks at no additional charge. Beyond that period, additional work is billed at our hourly rate or covered under a maintenance retainer.

8. Physical shipping

If an engagement involves physical deliverables — for example printed brand guidelines, signage, or packaging samples — these are shipped via TCS or Leopards Courier within Pakistan, with tracking provided. Shipping outside Pakistan is arranged via DHL or FedEx and is quoted separately. Standard delivery within Pakistan is 3 to 5 working days from despatch.
