[ 03 ] — Approach

How we work.

01

Listening,
before drawing.

Every engagement opens with a structured discovery phase — usually two to three weeks of stakeholder conversations, audit work, and reading. We've never solved a brand problem we didn't first take the time to understand.

02

Strategy
as a written thing.

We document. Positioning, audience, voice, visual direction — all of it written down before a single pixel moves. Strategy that lives only in someone's head doesn't survive the project.

03

Craft, in
a slow hand.

We work in small teams of two to three on each engagement. No account managers, no telephone. The people designing the work are the people you talk to about it. That's not a feature — it's the only way we know how to be good.

04

Care
that continues.

Most projects roll into a retainer. New brand systems need maintenance, websites need iteration, growth needs attention. We stay close to the brands we launch — that's where most of our time actually goes.

[ 04 ] — Studio

A small studio, by design.

We started ODQZ in 2024 because the work we wanted to do — slow, considered, in-house from strategy through code — wasn't happening at the agencies we'd worked at before.

Today we're a team of six designers, developers, and writers based out of a quiet studio in Gulberg, Lahore. We work with a deliberately small client list — typically four to six concurrent engagements — split between project work and ongoing retainers.

Most of our clients come through referral. We turn down more work than we take. That isn't false modesty; it's how we keep the quality where we want it.

If you're a founder, an in-house brand lead, or an operator at a company that takes its work seriously, we'd like to hear from you.

Brand identity engagements typically begin at PKR 350,000 and scale based on scope. Website projects begin at PKR 450,000. Ongoing retainers start at PKR 150,000 per month with a minimum six-month commitment. We send a detailed proposal after our first conversation — fixed fee, no hourly billing.
A focused brand identity is usually six to eight weeks. A marketing website runs eight to twelve. E-commerce or product work typically takes longer — twelve to sixteen weeks depending on integrations. We don't rush, and we don't pad. Timelines are written into the contract.
